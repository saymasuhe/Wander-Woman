Founder's Journey

Even as a child, our founder Sabira would often toddle out of her neighborhood alone. Her curiosity and passion for travel eventually led her to go beyond the borders of Bangladesh to over 25 countries today. But from the very beginning, she realized there was a problem. A big one.

Hence in 2017, she took the initiative to create a Facebook group to share her experiences and find others with similar experiences. The group quickly grew in members and stories. Sabira and her team of moderators worked tirelessly to provide accurate data and support for their members to travel in safety and comfort. All of which transformed the close-knit community you know today as “Wander Woman”.
